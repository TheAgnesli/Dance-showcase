Jazz Dance

What is it: Jazz dance is a performance dance and style that arose in the United States in the early 20th century. Jazz dance may allude to vernacular jazz, Broadway or dramatic jazz. The two types expand on African American vernacular styles of dance that arose with jazz music.

When was it created: Jazz dance originated in the early 20th century in African American communities in the U.S.

Hip-hop Dance

What is it: Hip-hop dance refers to street dance styles like breaking, popping, and locking, performed to hip-hop music.

When was it created: It originated in the 1970s in the Bronx, New York City, as part of the larger hip-hop culture.

Pop Dance

What is it: Pop dance is a modern dance style performed to popular music, characterized by upbeat and rhythmic movements.

When was it created: Pop dance became popular in the late 20th century with the rise of pop music and artists like Michael Jackson.
